## Recommended Learning Path

**Week 1 -- Foundations (Lessons 1-5)**
Start here. These cover the core language: variables, functions, control flow, data structures, and error handling. Complete all exercises before moving on.

**Week 2 -- Ownership and Abstraction (Lessons 6-9)**
These lessons cover what makes Fajar Lang unique: ownership, generics, traits, collections, and modules. Take extra time on Lesson 6 (ownership) -- it is the most important concept.

**Week 3 -- Domain Features (Lessons 10-14)**
Choose based on your interest. ML engineers should focus on Lesson 10. OS developers should focus on Lessons 11-12. Everyone should complete Lesson 14 (testing).
