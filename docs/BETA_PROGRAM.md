# Fajar Lang Beta Program

## Overview

We're looking for 3-5 early adopters to try Fajar Lang in real embedded AI projects and provide feedback on the language, compiler, and tooling.

## Ideal Beta Users

- **Embedded AI engineers** building drone, robot, or IoT systems
- **OS researchers** exploring AI-integrated kernels
- **Safety-critical teams** in automotive, aerospace, or medical devices
- **ML engineers** deploying models to edge hardware

## What You Get

- Direct access to the language designer for support
- Priority bug fixes and feature requests
- Early access to new features before public release
- Co-authorship opportunity on case study publications
- Your project featured in Fajar Lang documentation

## What We Ask

- Try Fajar Lang for a real (small) project or prototype
- Report bugs, friction points, and feature gaps
- Share performance measurements vs your current stack
- Allow us to write a case study about your experience (anonymized if preferred)
- 1-2 hours of feedback sessions over 4 weeks

## Application

Email beta@fajarlang.org with:
1. Your name/team and domain (drone, robot, IoT, OS, etc.)
2. Brief description of the project you'd try Fajar Lang for
3. Your current tech stack (C/C++/Rust/Python/etc.)
4. What interests you most about Fajar Lang

## Timeline

- **Week 1-2**: Setup, hello world, basic examples
- **Week 3-4**: Port a small component to Fajar Lang
- **Week 5-6**: Feedback sessions, bug reports
- **Week 7-8**: Case study draft, final evaluation
